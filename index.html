my story

She didn't find herself. She remembered her.

I'm Kelly — a certified life coach in Wilmington, NC, and the woman behind Garnet Hearth. I built this space because I know what it feels like to lose yourself inside the beautiful, exhausting work of becoming a mother. To look up one day and not quite recognize the woman in the mirror.


Garnet Hearth is the sanctuary I wish I'd had. A hearth table, not a wellness brochure. A place to sit down, breathe, and remember who you were before you gave everything away.

"You don't have to have it figured out to belong here. You just have to be ready to come home."
