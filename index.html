The Heart Behind the Hearth

Meet Kelly

I'm Kelly Pollard — certified health coach, mom, and the woman who spent a long time waiting to feel like herself again before realizing nobody was going to hand that back to me.


Garnet Hearth is the space I built because it didn't exist yet. A soft place to land when you're not sure who you are anymore. No fixing. No performing. Just an honest conversation and someone who has sat exactly where you're sitting.

"For the woman who has been everything for everyone, and is ready to come home to herself."
