A Note For You

Hello, Dearie.

If you're reading this, something brought you here. Maybe you Googled 'how to reset' at 2am. Maybe a friend sent it to you. Maybe you've been quietly carrying something for a long time and you just needed to put it down for a minute.

Whatever it is, come pull up a chair. The hearth is lit.

This isn't a wellness program. It's not a routine you're going to fail at. It's not one more thing you have to do perfectly.

It's seven small tools, for the moments that don't make it into anyone's Instagram feed. The bathroom-floor moments. The car-in-the-driveway moments. The 3am moments.

You don't have to read it in order. You don't have to memorize it. You don't even have to finish it. Find the moment that sounds like yours, and try the tool. That's the whole assignment.

And if it works, even just a tad, come find me. I'll be at the hearth.

Kelly

Reiki Master · Certified Life Coach · Garnet Hearth
